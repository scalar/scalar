## Naming

"Scalar" is a single word, always spelled with a capital *S*. It is the brand name of both our company and our platform, not "Scalar app" or "scalar.com". Refer to the platform as **Scalar**.

Individual products are proper nouns and should be capitalized when referred to by their full name: **Scalar Docs**, **Scalar API References**, **Scalar Registry**, **Scalar SDKs**, and **Scalar API Client**. When the context is already clear, the short form is fine (Docs, API References, Registry, SDKs, API Client).

---

## Usage

Give our assets room to breathe. Scale them up or down, but never stretch, recolor, outline, rotate, or layer effects on top of them. Keep at least the height of the logomark as clear space on every side so the mark stands on its own.

These graphics are proprietary and protected by intellectual property laws. Please do not alter the files, imply a relationship, affiliation, or endorsement by Scalar, or combine them with other marks without written consent. Have a question or need custom assets? [Get in touch](mailto:support@scalar.com).
